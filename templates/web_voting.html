Voice Voting Instructions

  1. Start Voting: Click the "Start Voice Voting" button to begin the process
  2. Voter ID: When prompted, clearly say "TEST1" or "TEST2"
  3. Candidate Selection: Listen to the candidates and say the number ("1", "2", or "3")
  4. Confirmation: Say "confirm" to cast your vote or "cancel" to abort
  5. Completion: Your vote will be recorded and you'll receive confirmation

Note: This system uses advanced AI for voice recognition. Speak clearly in a quiet environment for best results.
